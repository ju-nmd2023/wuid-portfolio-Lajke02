Yayoi Kusama Coffee table book spreads

The last graded assignment for my Fundamentals of Graphic Design course had to do with making 2 spreads of a coffee table book with the main focus being a designer. I choose Yayoi Kusama, the proclaimed princess of polkadots, who is famous all around the world for her recognizable style in her graphic design, illustrations, sculptures and more. To celebrate her as an artist I decided to follow a polkadot theme and a similar colorscheme with red, black and blue.
