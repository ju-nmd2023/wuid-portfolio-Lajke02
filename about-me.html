Me

Hi, I'm Lykke! I'm a 21 year old graphic design and web development student at JTH, Jönköping, Sweden. I'm interested in art, graphic design, music, nature and cats.

I grew up in a little town called Värnamo, which is about 1 hour from Jönköping.

I actually civics in highschool but chose an entirely different way to end up here. After highschool I worked several different jobs for 2 years to gain some experience and have a better financial standpoint as I started studying. The most boring situation for me in an work environment is be where I would be at a point that I could not learn any more from the work I'm doing. Which is why I've switched jobs quite a few times. To work with something more creative is also something I really enjoy doing.
